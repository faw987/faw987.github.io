Simple G-code Interpreter

This demo supports a small, readable subset of CNC-style G-code and animates the resulting toolpath on a 2D canvas.

Program

Supported subset: G0/G00 rapid move, G1/G01 linear cut move, G90 absolute mode, G91 relative mode, F feed rate metadata, and M2/M30 end program. Coordinates use X and Y. Semicolon comments are allowed.
